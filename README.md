# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I'm Raj, a third - year Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. I'm deeply passionate about the intersection of machine learning, data science and complex scientific domains like astrophysics and quantum computing. I enjoy building data - driven solutions using Python, C++, R and MATLAB, with my recent work ranging from a capstone project predicting Coronal Mass Ejections to developing a movie recommender system and a fantasy cricket prediction model. I am always open to collaborating on innovative tech projects.
